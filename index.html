About Me

I am a data storyteller with 8 years of experience designing and deploying ML solutions across the Medicare, Pharmaceutical, Retail and Insurance sectors. In my professional journey, I have developed and optimized machine learning models for medical imaging analysis, patient analytics, demand forecasting, risk prediction, claims automation translating business goals into scalable, reliable and explainable data driven systems. I am well versed in the full ML lifecycle from data engineering and model development to deployment and monitoring using Docker, Kubernetes and Airflow on AWS or private customer Kubernetes clusters, always aiming to deliver impactful and ethical solutions.

Alongside my professional work, I enjoy engaging in Kaggle competitions. They help me continuously learn, explore new techniques and I see competitions as a playground to test ideas, experiment with approaches and bring creative solutions into real world applications. Outside of work and competitions, I focus on healthcare oriented personal projects to deepen my domain expertise. I work on ECG signal analysis, medical text semantics, medical imaging and knowledge graph based reasoning. My goal is to build intelligent systems that turn complex clinical data into actionable insights, improve diagnostic accuracy and support data informed decision making in healthcare.
